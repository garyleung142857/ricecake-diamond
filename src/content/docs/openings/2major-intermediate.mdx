12-14. 5+M, no 4+oM. Unbal (at least some singleton or 6+M).

<Aside type="note">
The shape that can be resolved after 2M is limited. Patterns with 5+m or voids cannot be resolved fully. Consider opening those hands with 1!c.
</Aside>
