Strengths in Mode 0:
- NV: 10-13
- V: 14-16

May have 5-card major or 6-card minor

<BiddingTree>
1!n
- 2!c: Puppet to 2!d. "5+!d any strength, both minors GF, or a major invite"
    - (1): Weak in !d
    - (2): Inv with long !d
    - (3): Long !d SI
    - (4): 5+!d 4=M GF
    - (5): 5+4+ minors GF
    - (6): Three suited short M SI, or
    - (7): 4-5M inv (not 4-card flat)
- 2!d: Trans to 2!h. 5+!h. Include 5-5 majors inv and 5+!h 4=!s GF
- 2!h: Trans to 2!s. 5+!s. Not 5=!s inv
- 2!s: Size ask.
    - (1): Bal inv (may have a 4-card major if flat)
    - (2): Bal SI, or
    - (3): Inv with long !c
- 2!n: Trans to 3!c
    - (1): Weak in !c
    - (2): 5+!c 4=M GF, or
    - (3): Long !c SI
- 3!c: 5+!s 4=!h GF
- 3!d: 4=!h 4=!s GF
- 3M: 4=M COG. Usually 3-oM. May pull to 5m after 3!n
- 3!n: To play
- 4!c/!d: Long !h/!s GF+
- 4M: To play
- 4!n: Quantitative
</BiddingTree>

## 2!d/!h Transfers

### Normal Accepts

<BiddingTree>
1!n - 2!d/!h; 2M
- 2!s: 5-5 majors inv+ or 5+!h 4=!s GF. 2!n ask, then
    - 3!c: 5+!h 4=!s GF. Continue like [1!n - 3!c](#1N%20-%203%E2%99%A3)
    - 3!d: 5+!h 5+!s GF. Natural continuation
    - 3!h: 5-5 majors inv
- 2!n: Art. 5+M 4+!c GF
    - 3!c: Ask
        - 3!d: 5+!c. 3!h Ask Hi / lo short
        - 3!h/!s/!n: 4=!c. Hi / lo / no short
- 3!c: Art. 5+M 4=!d GF. 3!d ask short (hi / lo / no)
- 3!d: Art. 5+M 5+!d GF. 3!h Ask Hi / lo short
- 3M: 6+M Inv
- 3!n: 5=M COG. Disperse values. 3!n is still possible even with fit
- 3oM/4!c+: [Long M ST](#long-suit-slam-tries)
</BiddingTree>

### Confit

Responder has a balanced hand, investigates the number of controls and a fit for a slam.

<BiddingTree>
1!n - 2!s; 2!n/3!c - 3!d
- 3!h: 2-3 controls
    - 3!s: Ask again
        - 3!n: 2 controls
        - 4!c: 3 controls, 4+ suits. Bid up the line
    - 3!n: To play
    - 4!c+: 4+ suits. Bid up the line
- 3!s: 4 controls
- 3!n: 5 controls
- 4!c+: 6+ controls. 4+ suit. Bid up the line
</BiddingTree>

<Aside type="note">
The number of control is based on 10-13 range. For 14-16 1!n, add one control to the requirements.
</Aside>

Then the development follows the following rules:
- Any time the responder rebids NT, the opener must pass
- The opener must not raise the level unless fitting
- Both players bids suits up the line, rebidding shows 5
- Fit partner by a raise

## Modules

### Major-Minor Game Forcing

After responder shows 5M 4+m after 1!n - 2!d/!h; 2!h/!s - 2!n/3!c/3!d; shape ask, opener can continue as follows

<BiddingTree>
5+M 4+m continuation
- 4m: SI in m.
- 4om/oM: SI in M. Cuebid
- 3N/4M/5m: NF
</BiddingTree>

After responder shows 4=M 5+m, opener can continue as follows

<BiddingTree>
5+m 4=M continuation
- 3M: Fit M. May have m fit
- 3!n: No fit. NF
- 3!s/4m: Fit m.
</BiddingTree>

### Long Suit Slam Tries

After a transfer to a suit, responder can initiate long suit ST by bidding 3!s (or 3!h after !s), 3!n in a minor, or 4-level bids.

<BiddingTree>
Long suit slam tries (Major) and continuation
- 3oM:  ST. No short (may have singleton Ace / King)
    - 4M: Negative
    - 3!n: Ask weak doubleton (worse than Jx)
        - 4m: m
        - 4M: oM
        - 4!n: No
- 4X: Splinter
- 4M: Mild ST. NF
- 4!n: RKCB
</BiddingTree>

<BiddingTree>
Long suit slam tries (Minor) and continuation
- 3!s: ST. No short (may have singleton Ace / King) 
    - 3!n: Negative
    - 4m: Nat ST
    - 4om: Ask weak doubleton (worse than Jx)
        - 4!d / 5!c: (Original suit) om
        - 4M: M
        - 4!n: No
- 3!n: Mild ST. NF
- 4m: (Responder's suit) Kick back RKCB
- 4X: Splinter
</BiddingTree>
