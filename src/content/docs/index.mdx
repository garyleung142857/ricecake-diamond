<b>Rice Cake Diamond (大福階) </b> bridge bidding system is suitable for you if you like:

<Card title="Notable references used in the system:" icon="document">
- [Magic Diamond Light](http://www.brenning.se/pp/MagicDlight.pdf) by Sven-Olov Flodqvist and Tomas Brenning
    - Strong influence on system structure
- [Infernal Machine](https://usbf.org/docs/2019usbc/acblcards/2019_USBF_Open_USBC_Dinkin_Shuster_System_Notes.pdf) by Sam Dinkin
    - Inspiration on continuations of cornerstone bids
- [Competitive Bids the Scanian Way](http://www.itbolaget.nu/bridge/competitivebids.asp) by Mats Nilsland
    - Inspiration on competitive sequence developments
- [Heeman](https://www.jackbridge.com/pdf/eheeman.pdf) by Wim Heemskerk
    - Inspiration on 1NT system
- [KK Relay](https://www.bridgeworld.com/pages/bookstore/itemdetailpages/itemdetailpage_2320.html) by Karen McCallum and Kit Woolsey
    - Reference for symmetric relay
