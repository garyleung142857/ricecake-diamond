2!n balanced is not used as an opening. Instead, it is used in the follow situations:

- 1!d - 1!h; 2!n
- 1!d - 1!h; 1!s - 1!n; 2!n
- (2X = Natural / weak) 2!n
- (2X = Natural / weak) P (P) 2!n
- Any modes of the above sequences where 2!n bidder can be strong

<BiddingTree>
2!n
- 3!c: Ask 5-card major
- 3!d: 5+!h. Transfer to 3!h. May have 4=!s
- 3!h: 5+!s. No 4+!h.
- 3!s: Puppet to 3!n. Either to play in 3!n, or minors ST
- 3!n: Art. 5=!s 4=!h COG
- 4!c/!d: Long !h / !s
- 4!h/!s: Long !c / !d ST
- 4!n: Inv to 6!n
- 5m: To play
</BiddingTree>
