Not being used in free-run bidding, but used in the following sequences:
- 1M (Dbl) 2!n
- (1X) 1M (...) 2!n
- (1!s) 2!h (P/Dbl) 2!n

<Aside type="note">
8+ card fit is assured, and we don't play at 3!n except direct 
</Aside>

- NS: Help suit game try / SI

- JS: Splinter
