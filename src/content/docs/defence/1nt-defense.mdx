- 2M: 5+M Nat
- 2!n: 5+!c 5+!d
- 3X: Preemptive

<Aside type="note">
Applies for direct seat, pass-out seat, passed hand, or any modes when opponents interfere with a natural 1!n and we have not shown a real suit.

Bid agressively (4-4 okay) opposite strong NT NV. Bid constructively (rule of 20) opposite weak NT.

Dbl denies a good 5-card !s suit. 2m denies a good 5-card !h suit

Third seat redouble is ignored.
</Aside>
